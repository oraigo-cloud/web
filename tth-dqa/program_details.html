Data Quality Analyst — Training-to-Hire Program

Career accelerator for entry-level Data Quality Analysts — 6 weeks | Project-first | Hire-ready

Why this program?

We train, evaluate and place talent. This hands-on program combines instructor-led training, a real project, mentorship and a hiring pathway with DataSphere Academy or partner clients.

Two flexible modes

  • Mode 1 — Training + Internship (Paid Training Mode): 6-week structured training followed by internship. Fee: ₹9,500.
  • Mode 2 — Direct Internship (No Training Fee): Apply directly for internship assignments (no training fee).

What you'll learn (hands-on)

  • Data quality fundamentals: accuracy, completeness, consistency, validity & timeliness
  • Data cleaning & profiling using Excel / Google Sheets and Power Query
  • SQL for validation & integrity checks (SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY, WHERE)
  • Data quality reporting & dashboarding (Excel / Power BI basics)
  • Documentation, stakeholder communication & best practices

Program flow (6 weeks)

  1. Week 0 — Screening & Onboarding: Aptitude test & selection, enrollment.
  2. Weeks 1–2 — Foundation Training: Core concepts, Excel & SQL basics, mini tasks.
  3. Weeks 3–5 — Project Simulation: Real dataset, cleaning, validation, dashboard & report.
  4. Week 6 — Evaluation & Placement: Final review, certification & hiring shortlist.

Outcomes & benefits

  • Real project to showcase on your portfolio / LinkedIn.
  • 1:1 mentorship and performance feedback.
  • Certificate on successful completion.
  • Top performers considered for full-time roles or client placements.
Eligibility

Open to graduates, final-year students and career switchers with strong attention to detail. Basic Excel is helpful; SQL is a plus but not mandatory.
