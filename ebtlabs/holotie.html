Executive Summary

EBT Labs proposes a customised training partnership with HoloTie to upskill your team in critical technology areas. Our approach emphasises hands-on learning, project deliverables and measurable outcomes. Below we outline our experience, proposed scope, delivery approach and the trainings we have conducted so far.

Proposed Scope & Format

  • Modular programmes (4–12 weeks) tailored to your needs.
  • Delivery options: on-site, live online, or blended.
  • Each module includes lectures, hands-on labs, mini projects, assessments and certificates.
  • Custom curriculum mapping to your tech stack and business objectives.
  • Maximum students permitted per batch: 30 (same year / semester).

Outcomes & Certification

Participants will complete assessed projects and receive a certificate of completion. We also supply a post-training report summarising learning outcomes, sample project code and recommendations for next steps.
