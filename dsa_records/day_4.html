Week 1 – Day 4: Engagement & Lead Nurturing

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training

Objective

To strengthen communication consistency by nurturing leads and building long-term engagement through follow-ups and online interactions.

Task Breakdown

  • Follow up with leads contacted on Day 3 and update their current status.
  • Send personalized follow-up messages or emails to potential customers who showed interest.
  • Engage with business profiles on social media — like, comment, and respond meaningfully to queries.
  • Create 2–3 short engagement messages that can be reused for follow-ups.
  • Discuss strategies to convert warm leads into active customers.
  • Maintain a clear record of all communication activities in the daily log.

Tools & Resources

  • Social media pages (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook)
  • Follow-up email/message templates
  • Lead tracker sheet (with notes on communication and response)

End-of-Day Checklist

  • ✅ All Day 3 leads have been followed up.
  • ✅ Communication logs updated with feedback and outcomes.
  • ✅ Minimum of 3 engagement messages created for future use.
  • ✅ Daily report submitted to the team lead.

Supervision Notes

  • Saksham (Team Lead): Ensure that follow-ups are genuine, professional, and well-documented. Provide samples of effective engagement communication.
  • Shahni (HR Oversight): Review that engagement logs are properly filled and support the lead in maintaining consistency.
