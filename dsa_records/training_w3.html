Week 3 – Advanced Campaign Execution & Client Interaction

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training Program

Overall Objective

To develop the intern’s ability to manage advanced marketing operations — running campaigns independently, engaging clients professionally, and using data analytics to optimize performance.

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1 – Campaign Launch & Monitoring

  • Plan and launch a small-scale marketing campaign using Week 2 message strategies.
  • Set up daily tracking in the CRM and Google Sheets.
  • Analyze engagement and conversion data in real time.
  • Discuss adjustments and strategy refinements with the team lead.

Team Lead Action: Review campaign setup, validate lead sources, and monitor entries.

Day 2 – Client Communication & Feedback Handling

  • Learn professional client communication practices.
  • Simulate live client interactions to handle FAQs or objections.
  • Follow up with at least 5 existing leads to collect feedback.
  • Summarize and categorize client feedback for analysis.

Team Lead Action: Conduct mock client sessions and share improved communication templates.

Day 3 – Marketing Analytics & Insight Generation

  • Use Google Sheets or dashboards to analyze campaign performance.
  • Identify high-performing audience segments and message types.
  • Prepare a data summary highlighting key patterns and insights.

Team Lead Action: Guide trainees on interpreting data and refining targeting approaches.

Day 4 – Collaboration & Creative Ideation

  • Brainstorm 2–3 creative content ideas (posts, taglines, short videos).
  • Present concepts to the team lead for feedback.
  • Understand how creativity influences brand visibility and conversions.

Team Lead Action: Review submissions and approve at least one idea for trial use.

Day 5 – Reporting & Presentation

  • Compile a weekly analytics report summarizing conversion trends and learnings.
  • Prepare a 3–5 minute presentation covering week’s work and insights.
  • Discuss improvement areas and define goals for Week 4.

Team Lead Action: Conduct the weekly review and forward the report to HR.

Tools & Resources

  • CRM / Google Sheets (for data tracking)
  • Social Media Platforms (for engagement monitoring)
  • Client Communication Templates (email, call scripts)
  • Weekly Analytics Report Format
