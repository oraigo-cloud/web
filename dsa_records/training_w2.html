Week 2 – Marketing & Conversion Growth Focus

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training Program

Overall Objective

To strengthen the trainee’s real-world marketing execution by improving customer engagement, increasing conversion consistency, and introducing campaign-level tracking and reporting.

Day-by-Day Plan

Day 1 – Campaign Structuring & Message Refinement

  • Review performance metrics from Week 1 conversions.
  • Identify target audience segments based on product relevance.
  • Create outreach message variations (A/B testing).
  • Update CRM / Lead Tracker with new campaign columns.

Team Lead Action: Validate message tone and ensure alignment with product goals.

Day 2 – Social Media Marketing & Engagement

  • Post brand-relevant content on official handles (LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.).
  • Engage with comments, messages, and business communities.
  • Track engagement metrics and report daily summary.

Team Lead Action: Review engagement responses, guide on community etiquette, and suggest improvements.

Day 3 – Calling & Conversion Optimization

  • Make 20+ calls using the updated pitch.
  • Collect feedback from at least 5 leads on pricing or value perception.
  • Practice objection-handling scenarios with the team lead.
  • Log all calls in the tracker with remarks and outcomes.

Team Lead Action: Conduct a 15-minute call review session with each trainee.

Day 4 – Competitor Analysis & Value Proposition

  • Research 3–4 competitors offering similar services.
  • Compare pricing, features, and marketing methods.
  • Prepare a one-page summary of DataSphere’s differentiators.

Team Lead Action: Review and refine talking points for next week’s campaigns.

Day 5 – Weekly Reporting & Strategy Review

  • Compile leads contacted, conversions achieved, and engagement data.
  • Create a learning summary of what worked and what needs improvement.
  • Attend review meeting with HR (Shahni) and Team Lead (Saksham).

Team Lead Action: Present weekly summary to HR and identify next-week goals.

Tools & Resources

  • Lead Tracker (Google Sheet)
  • CRM Access (if available)
  • Social Media Dashboards (Meta Business Suite, LinkedIn)
  • Call Logs / Feedback Sheets
  • Competitor Research Template

Evaluation Metrics

Parameter Target Evaluated By
Calls Made 20 per day Saksham
Conversions 2–3 per day Saksham
Engagement Activity 2 posts/interactions daily Shahni
Report Submission Weekly HR Department
