DataSphere Academy — Marketing Internship

Week 1: Structured training plan for Marketing Intern (Brand promotion, Social Engagement, Calling & Conversions)

Program: LiteSites & General Marketing | Duration: Week 1 (5 working days)

Objective

By the end of Week 1, the trainee will be familiar with DataSphere's brand messaging, be able to engage potential customers on social media, handle basic queries, and perform outreach calls with a daily conversion target. The Team Lead will guide, review and provide feedback daily.

Daily Plan (Trainee & Team Lead)

Day 1 — Orientation & Foundations

Trainee
  • Attend onboarding meeting with Team Lead (30–45 mins).
  • Study product brief (LiteSites) and key marketing collateral.
  • Learn target customer profile & ideal use-cases.
  • Task: Write a 100–150 word promotion summary for LiteSites and submit to Team Lead.
Team Lead
  • Conduct product & marketing strategy overview.
  • Share templates: outreach script, social responses, lead tracker.
  • Review trainee's summary and give corrective feedback.

Day 2 — Social Media Promotion & Engagement

Trainee
  • Identify 20 potential leads on LinkedIn / Instagram / Facebook.
  • Make at least 10 meaningful engagements (comments, shares, DMs where appropriate).
  • Create 2 short promotional posts/messages for LiteSites (use provided template).
  • Log all interactions in the lead tracker sheet.
Team Lead
  • Review engagement tone and messaging for brand consistency.
  • Provide feedback on the 2 promotional posts; approve or suggest edits.
  • Ensure lead tracker is correctly populated.

Day 3 — Customer Query Handling & Templates

Trainee
  • Study common customer queries and existing FAQs.
  • Practice 6 simulated chat/email responses (use templates).
  • Note unclear questions and prepare 3–5 clarifying questions for the Team Lead.
Team Lead
  • Run a mock Q&A session (role play customer interactions).
  • Review trainee replies and refine templates where needed.
  • Approve 2–3 response templates for live use.

Day 4 — Outreach Calls (LiteSites focus)

Trainee
  • Make 10–20 calls to potential businesses (use call script).
  • Record each call in the tracker: business name, contact, outcome, next action.
  • Daily target: aim for 2–3 conversions (or qualified follow-ups) per day.
  • Log objections encountered and top 5 questions from prospects.
Team Lead
  • Monitor call logs and provide on-the-spot coaching during the calling session.
  • Review recordings or summaries and suggest pitch improvements.
  • Help reassign follow-ups and approve conversion entries.

Day 5 — Weekly Review & Next Steps

Trainee
  • Prepare a Weekly Summary Report (template provided): calls made, social engagements, conversions, challenges, learning points.
  • Present a 10-minute recap to Team Lead in the review meeting.
  • Set personal goals for Week 2 based on feedback.
Team Lead
  • Conduct 1:1 review meeting — discuss performance and areas for improvement.
  • Provide a written feedback note and Week 2 objectives.
  • Decide whether additional coaching is required or if the trainee can begin independent outreach.
