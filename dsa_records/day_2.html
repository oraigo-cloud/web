Week 1 – Day 2: Platform Familiarization & Communication Practice

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training

Objective

To familiarize the trainee with DataSphere’s communication tools, social platforms, and reporting structure. To develop confident and professional communication skills through guided practice.

Task Breakdown

  • Introduce the trainee to official tools: CRM/lead tracker, social media platforms, and internal communication channels.
  • Demonstrate how to record leads, update statuses, and report progress daily.
  • Practice social engagement – responding to customer comments and DMs professionally.
  • Conduct short roleplay sessions for outreach calls and social replies to build confidence and tone consistency.
  • End the day with a 15-minute review to provide communication feedback and note improvement areas.

Tools & Resources

  • Google Sheets / Lead Tracker File
  • Official company social media pages (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook)
  • Training templates: Outreach Script and Social Response Samples

End-of-Day Checklist

  • ✅ Trainee understands use of communication tools and trackers.
  • ✅ Completed communication roleplay and feedback received.
  • ✅ Updated tracker shared with team lead.

Supervision Notes

  • Saksham (Team Lead): Ensure the trainee is comfortable using all tools and maintains clarity in daily updates.
  • Shahni (HR Oversight): Monitor training flow and provide support for any technical or communication issues faced by Saksham or the trainee.
