Week 1 – Day 3: Outreach & Calling Practice

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training

Objective

To help the trainee develop confidence in outbound communication, understand the customer pitch, and begin active lead outreach with measurable daily goals.

Task Breakdown

  • Provide the trainee with a target list of 10–15 small businesses for outreach.
  • Have the trainee make 10–20 introductory calls using the official outreach script.
  • Explain and promote the LiteSites plan (₹100/month) clearly to potential clients.
  • Record outcomes for each call in the lead tracker sheet (status, remarks, follow-up date).
  • Review 2–3 call summaries or recordings for quality and clarity.
  • Offer constructive feedback on communication tone, product explanation, and handling objections.

Tools & Resources

  • Lead Tracker Sheet (Google Sheets or Excel)
  • Outreach Script (official template)
  • CRM or contact list for calling

End-of-Day Checklist

  • ✅ Minimum of 10–20 calls completed and logged.
  • ✅ Lead tracker updated with remarks and follow-up notes.
  • ✅ Reviewed and discussed 2–3 sample calls with team lead.

Supervision Notes

  • Saksham (Team Lead): Ensure the trainee follows the script confidently, maintains professionalism, and records all data accurately.
  • Shahni (HR Oversight): Verify that outreach is being tracked properly and provide process support if any platform issues arise.
