Week 1 – Day 5: Conversion Focus & Weekly Reporting

DataSphere Academy – Marketing Internship Training

Objective

To consolidate all learnings from the first week, emphasize conversion-oriented communication, and generate a structured progress report for evaluation.

Task Breakdown

  • Focus on converting warm leads contacted during the week into confirmed customers.
  • Attempt at least 10 follow-up calls or messages with a goal of 2–3 confirmed conversions.
  • Review communication strategy and identify which scripts or approaches worked best.
  • Prepare a summary report including total leads reached, responses received, and conversions achieved.
  • Participate in an end-of-week feedback session with the team lead for progress review and next steps.

Tools & Resources

  • Lead Tracker Sheet (updated with conversion status)
  • Call scripts, message templates, and sample pitch decks
  • Google Docs / Sheets for weekly reporting

End-of-Day Checklist

  • ✅ Completed final round of calls and follow-ups.
  • ✅ At least 2–3 conversions achieved or documented with feedback.
  • ✅ Weekly report compiled and shared with the team lead.
  • ✅ Attended final review session with HR and lead.

Supervision Notes

  • Saksham (Team Lead): Review the overall performance metrics, provide constructive feedback, and plan the next week’s targets accordingly.
  • Shahni (HR Oversight): Ensure that the weekly report and progress documentation are received and stored for HR evaluation.
