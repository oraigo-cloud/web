Marketing Internship Training – Day 1

Objective

Introduce the trainee to DataSphere Academy’s marketing strategy, product knowledge, and communication framework. Provide practical tools and templates to begin initial outreach.

Tasks for the Team Lead

  • Conduct a detailed Product & Marketing Strategy Overview session (covering target audience, brand positioning, and communication channels).
  • Share essential resources with the trainee:
    • Outreach Script – for calling and messaging leads.
    • Social Response Templates – for engaging potential customers online.
    • Lead Tracker Sheet – to log all interactions and responses.
  • Review the trainee’s written summary on “How to promote LiteSites effectively online.”
  • Provide constructive feedback and improvement points for tone, clarity, and professionalism.

Tasks for the Trainee

  • Attend the orientation and strategy session led by the team lead.
  • Review shared templates and understand their use in daily marketing activities.
  • Write a short (100–150 words) summary describing how you would promote LiteSites to small businesses online.
  • Submit the summary for review and feedback by the end of the day.

Expected Outcome

  • Trainee gains a clear understanding of company products and marketing direction.
  • Establishment of communication workflow between trainee and team lead.
  • Completion of first written marketing task with guided feedback.
